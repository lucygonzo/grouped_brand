# Photography

Photography in Grouped is intimate, high-contrast, and warm. It shows artists in their element: performing, creating, connecting with fans. Every image should feel like a moment worth remembering.


## Treatments

Three core post-production treatments that define the Grouped photographic style:

| Treatment | Description |
|---|---|
| **Warm color grade** | Push toward warm tones. Color grade should complement the bronze accent. |
| **Slight desaturation** | Pull back 10-20% from natural. Let the bronze accent be the warmest thing in any composition. Skin tones stay natural. |
| **Dark vignette edges** | Edge vignetting helps photos sit cleanly on dark surfaces without harsh borders or visible crop lines. |


## Post-Production Specs

| Property | Specification |
|---|---|
| **Color Temperature** | Warm bias. Push toward 5500-6500K. Shadows lean slightly warm (amber/brown), highlights lean neutral. |
| **Saturation** | Pull back 10-20% from natural. Let bronze accent be the warmest element. Skin tones stay natural. |
| **Contrast** | High. Lift blacks slightly (to around 5-10%) so shadows have detail but stay rich. Highlights can blow out in stage lighting contexts. |
| **Grain** | Optional. Subtle film grain (ISO 400-800 equivalent) adds texture for editorial contexts. Skip grain in product screenshots and clean UI shots. |


## Do

- **High contrast, warm tones** -- Deep blacks, warm highlights. Color grade should complement the bronze accent. Slightly desaturated to keep the palette cohesive. Think film stock, not phone camera.
- **Tight crops, real energy** -- Close-up and medium shots. Show hands on instruments, faces in crowds, studio sessions in progress. Intimacy over spectacle. Movement over posed stills.
- **Dark vignetting, low-key lighting** -- Images blend into dark backgrounds naturally. Edge vignetting helps photos sit cleanly on dark surfaces without harsh borders or visible crop lines.
- **Natural and ambient light** -- Stage lighting, studio lamps, golden hour. The light source should feel contextual, not manufactured. Embrace shadow and negative space within the frame.
- **Diverse genres and settings** -- Grouped serves all artists. Photography should represent hip-hop producers, indie singer-songwriters, DJs, R&B vocalists, bands in rehearsal rooms. Varied, always authentic.
- **Fan perspective shots** -- Include the fan side: crowd shots from the artist's POV, phones held up at shows, merch tables, listening sessions. This is a two-sided platform. Show both sides.


## Don't

- **No stock photography** -- No generic studio portraits with flat lighting. No "musician smiling at camera" stock imagery. If we don't have the right photo, use the ring motif or type treatment instead.
- **No white/light device frames** -- Product mockups always use dark device frames on dark backgrounds. White device frames break the visual language. Screenshots should reflect the app's native dark mode.
- **No oversaturated or filtered images** -- No Instagram-style presets, no VSCO film emulations, no heavy color grading that distorts skin tones. Warmth comes from lighting and subtle post, not from filters layered on top.


## Cross-Surface Translation

| Surface | How Photography Shows Up |
|---|---|
| **Product (app)** | Artist avatars, album art, campaign cover images. Dark vignette treatment applied. |
| **Marketing site** | Hero imagery, feature section backgrounds, artist spotlights. Full treatment applied. |
| **Mobile** | Same treatments, cropped tighter for small viewports. Vignette essential for edge blending. |
| **Print** | CMYK conversion of warm grade. Proof warmth on press -- process can shift warm tones. |
| **Events/experiential** | Large-format prints on dark backdrops. Desaturation keeps imagery cohesive with brand palette. |
| **Social** | Artist-provided imagery with Grouped warm grade applied. Square and 4:5 crops. |
| **Email** | Header hero images only. Compressed JPEGs with dark vignette for edge blending. |
| **Investor materials** | Curated artist shots that show platform use cases. Full treatment, high production value. |
